## Story Points

These are my recommended story points for determining the level of effort for a Jira ticket / task.

| Point Value | Effort Expected |
|---|---|
| 1 | Half a day or less | 
| 2 | One day |  
| 3 | Two the three days | 
| 5 | One week |
| 8 | One sprint |
| 13 | Not enough information |
